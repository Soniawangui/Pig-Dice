OBJECTIVES

The Objective of Pig Dice Game is to score 100 points before the other player does.The Pig dice game is played by a six-sided dice.

GAMEPLAY

  • In each turn a player rolls a dice repeatedly until:either a 1 is rolled or player decides to hold and pass on the turn to his/her opponent.
  • The number the player rolls is added to a temporary score. If the player rolls a 1,they lose their temporary score and the turn is passed on the to the other opponent.
  • If the player gets any other number,they can decide to continue rolling the dice.
  • If the player decides to hold,then their total is added to the overall score.
  • The first player to score 100 points wins the game.
